# World Cup squads for selected teams: 1994, 1998, 2002

This document compiles the full tournament squads for the requested selected teams from the 1994, 1998, and 2002 FIFA World Cups. Squad rosters are taken from the tournament squad pages, and semifinalist status is taken from the tournament summary pages.[cite:1][cite:2][cite:4][cite:5]

## Selection scope

For 1994, the selected semifinalists are Brazil, Bulgaria, Italy, and Sweden.[cite:4]

For 1998, the selected semifinalists are Brazil, Croatia, France, and the Netherlands.[cite:2]

For 2002, the selected semifinalists are Brazil, Germany, South Korea, and Turkey.[cite:5]
